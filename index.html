Ehsaas Program 8171: How to Check Eligibility & Register Online

The Ehsaas Program 8171 is a major initiative by the Government of Pakistan to support low-income families. This guide helps you understand how to check your eligibility, register, and receive financial assistance.

What is the Ehsaas Program 8171?

Launched in 2019, the Ehsaas Program is Pakistan’s largest social protection initiative. The Ehsaas Program 8171 component specifically provides cash transfers to families in need.

How to Check Eligibility for Ehsaas Program 8171

To check your eligibility, follow these steps:

  • Visit www.8171.pass.gov.pk
  • Enter your CNIC number
  • Solve the CAPTCHA
  • Click "Submit"

Alternatively, you can send your CNIC number via SMS to 8171.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Monthly income below Rs. 25,000
  • Unemployed or no formal business
  • Not a government employee
  • Not receiving other BISP aid
  • Special groups include widows, orphans, and the disabled

How to Register for the Ehsaas Program 8171

Online Registration

Visit the official portal, complete the registration form and submit it.

In-Person Registration

Visit your local Ehsaas or BISP center with your CNIC and any required documents. Assistance will be provided onsite.

Payment Collection Methods

  • BVS agents with CNIC
  • ATM withdrawals using Ehsaas card
  • Designated bank branches
  • Mobile wallet services (where applicable)

Additional Tips

  • Keep your CNIC updated and valid
  • Update contact and bank info as needed
  • Call 0800-26477 for help or follow-up

Research & Impact

According to the Pakistan Centre for Philanthropy, Ehsaas has supported over 15 million families with emergency cash payments since its inception. The Ehsaas Program 8171 plays a critical role in improving financial stability and reducing poverty levels (PCP.org.pk).

Conclusion

The Ehsaas Program 8171 offers much-needed support to Pakistan’s most vulnerable. Check your eligibility, complete the registration, and choose a payment method that suits you best. Stay informed through the official Ehsaas portal.
